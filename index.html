📱 How to Play

1️⃣ Set Up Your Game

  • Create a private room or join a public match.
  • Customize your character pool or use the default set.
  • Each player gets a secret character—keep it hidden!

2️⃣ Ask Smart Questions

  • Take turns asking yes/no questions to narrow down possibilities.
  • "Does your character wear glasses?"
  • "Is your character fictional?"
  • Use process of elimination to rule out options.

3️⃣ Make Your Guess!

  • When you’re confident, take a shot at guessing the opponent’s character.
  • Guess correctly to win—but be careful, a wrong guess means you lose!
