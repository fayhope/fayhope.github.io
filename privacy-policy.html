Privacy Policy for Guess? App

Last Updated: 31/03/2025

Welcome to Guess? (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services. By accessing or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

A. Gameplay Data

To improve the App, we may collect:

  • Game progress & stats (wins and losses)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Analytics (session length, feature usage, crash reports)

B. User-Generated Content

If you upload custom images (e.g., for characters), we store:

  • Photos (processed for gameplay only and removed after every game is complete)
  • Metadata (file type, upload time)

C. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Firebase (Google) – For authentication, database, and analytics. Your images and the names of your contacts will be stored on the cloud for the duration of the game and then be removed.
  • AdMob – For displaying ads

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • ✔ Provide and improve the App (matchmaking, bug fixes)
  • ✔ Personalise your experience (characters, names)
  • ✔ Send updates (notifications, announcements)
  • ✔ Analyze trends (optimize gameplay, server performance)

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Other players (usernames, in-game actions, uploaded images in matches)
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Legal authorities (if required by law or to protect rights/safety)

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Game data is stored for up to 1 month after playing
  • Uploaded images are removed as soon as the game they are used in is over
  • Aggregated analytics may be kept indefinitely for research

To request deletion, contact us at: fayhope04@icloud.com.

5. Security Measures

  • Encryption (data in transit via HTTPS)
  • Access controls (limited employee access to sensitive data)
  • Regular audits (security vulnerability checks)

6. Children’s Privacy

  • The App is not intended for children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13.
  • Parents/guardians may contact us to remove a child’s data.

7. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in countries with different privacy laws. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Disable analytics tracking (via device settings)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Check the "Last Updated" date for changes. Continued use = acceptance.

10. Contact Us
